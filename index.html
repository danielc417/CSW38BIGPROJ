Bouncing Particle System

by Daniel Carranza • CSW 38

Canvas Demo

Watch the particles bounce around the canvas. Each circle has its own random size, color, and speed.

Reflection

This project helped me understand how the animation loop works with requestAnimationFrame(), how to create a reusable Particle class, and how to detect and respond to collisions with the canvas edges. Randomizing the radius, color, and velocity made the system feel more alive.
